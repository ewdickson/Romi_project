Bump Sensor
===========

The selected bump sensor is a ** . It should be noted that we only have one sensor of the pair 
sold online, as teams were scavenging from previous quarters' students (many thanks to Katherine
Meezan for her generous donation). We mounted the sensor such that at least one of its three 
"arms" were centered over the front of the Romi chassis. Since it did not matter to us which of
the three sensors were hit, we soldered the three channels together to simplify the electronics
and reduce the number of pins used for detecting bumps.
